definition

impulse control is the ability to pause and think before you act on something you want to do right away.

why do we need impulse control?

it helps us stay safe, respectful, and make better choices. without it, we might hurt ourselves or others, or get into trouble.

the brain science

the prefrontal cortex helps with decision-making and control. when it’s still growing (like at our age), it's harder to stop and think. impulse control gets better as we grow.

what does it affect?

impulse control affects school behavior, friendships, family life, safety, and emotions.

examples

your friends don’t let you play, and you steal the ball. is that using impulse control? ❌ no
someone cuts in line and you tell the teacher instead of yelling. is that impulse control? ✅ yes

pros & cons

pros: better relationships, fewer consequences, more respect, less stress

cons: takes patience, can feel hard in the moment
