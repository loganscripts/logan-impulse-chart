logan’s impulse chart

a guide to understanding and mastering impulse control

what is impulse control?

impulse control means stopping yourself from doing something right away and thinking before you act.

why do we need it?

it helps us make smart choices, avoid trouble, and build strong relationships.

how the brain is involved

the prefrontal cortex (right behind your forehead) helps you think before you act. it grows stronger with practice.

examples

  • your friends don’t let you play so you steal the ball. is that impulse control?
  • you want to yell at your sibling but take a breath instead. impulse control?

pros and cons

pros: smarter choices, better friendships, safer actions

cons: too much control can make you hold in your feelings
