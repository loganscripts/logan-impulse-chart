strategies to improve impulse control

take deep breaths

count to 5 and slowly breathe in and out to calm your brain.

walk away

removing yourself from a situation gives you time to think.

journal your emotions

writing down feelings helps you understand what triggers you.

use impulse control apps

apps like “headspace” or “breathe” can help you stay in control.
